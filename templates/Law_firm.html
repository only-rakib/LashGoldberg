Who We Are – About Lash & Goldberg LLP

An Experienced Litigation Law Firm

With offices in Miami, Ft. Lauderdale, and Tampa, Florida, Lash & Goldberg LLP represents business interests in litigation matters within Florida and throughout the United States. The Firm handles a broad range of litigation cases, from complex commercial matters to class actions and qui tam and white collar criminal defense. The Firm also counsels companies on administrative and regulatory matters and conducts corporate compliance reviews and investigations.

Founded in 1996, Lash & Goldberg LLP has developed a focused litigation practice. From a well-established foundation of knowledge and understanding of our clients’ business interests, the Firm assists corporate clients with litigation challenges in all facets of their business and operations. With years of trial experience, the Firm’s lawyers are able to simplify and effectively convey the nuances of complex business disputes to state and federal court judges and juries, arbitration panels, administrative hearing officers, and in various appellate forums throughout the country.

The Firm has demonstrated a depth and breadth of experience and bench strength by representing some of the nation’s largest companies for decades, and litigating with and against some of the world’s largest law firms. Along with a robust litigation practice, we pledge and maintain the highest degree of flexibility and personal service any client would want from their counsel and law firm.

Our Practice Areas

We practice in the following areas:

  • Alternative Dispute Resolution - Arbitration and mediation often provide the most efficient, cost-effective method for resolving conflict. Lash & Goldberg LLP regularly resolves disputes outside of the traditional courtroom setting through established Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) organizations, including the American Arbitration Association, JAMS and the American Health Lawyers Association ADR Service.
  • Complex Business Litigation - The Firm handles federal and state court claims ranging from breach of contract to all forms of business torts.
  • Class Action Litigation - Lash & Goldberg LLP has the substantial experience and extensive resources necessary to effectively litigate regional and national class actions. We handle class actions arising in a wide range of industries involving a variety of substantive issues.
  • Corporate, Partnership & Shareholder Disputes - Disputes that arise between partners, principals or officers and directors can disrupt operations and jeopardize business relationships. Lash & Goldberg LLP helps resolve corporate, partnership and shareholder issues discretely, quickly and appropriately to avoid unnecessary disruption in business. We defend public and private companies and their officers and directors in lawsuits brought by shareholders and partners.
  • Employment Litigation - We defend corporate employers in contract disputes, discrimination and disability claims, whistleblower actions, and all other forms of workplace grievances.
  • Health Care Operations & Litigation - Lash & Goldberg LLP assists health care institutions with legal issues that affect payment, regulatory compliance, government oversight and other complex matters unique to the health care industry. Our clients include health systems, hospitals and large corporate multispecialty provider groups.
  • Real Estate Litigation – The Firm represents clients in a variety of real estate litigation matters, including those arising out of raw land transactions, construction projects, commercial landlord-tenant relationships, workouts and loan restructurings.
  • Sports & Entertainment Litigation – We understand the unique interplay between law and business in the sports and entertainment industries. We have represented athletes, journalists, agents and broadcasters in a variety of complex commercial litigation matters.
  • Consumer Litigation Defense – Our representation includes defense of both individual and class action claims alleging a violation of consumer protection laws. As this landscape evolves, the Firm's lawyers stay abreast of technological developments that create legal implications for our business clients.
  • Government Investigations, False Claims Act & White Collar Criminal Defense - Stricter federal regulations and intensified law enforcement have prompted an increase in the number of investigations of companies and their executives. Our attorneys counsel clients who face criminal indictment and related civil and administrative challenges at the federal, state and local levels. We act quickly and firmly at all stages of the process — from investigation to trial and appeal.
  • Appellate Advocacy – We handle appeals at the federal and state level in a variety of matters involving corporate and partnership disputes, real estate litigation, employment claims, business torts and commercial disputes. Our appeals often involve novel procedural or substantive issues.
